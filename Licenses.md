## 3. How licences are used in Power BI

Once a user has a licence assigned:

- They can use the visual in **any workspace** within your tenant.
- They can use the visual in **multiple reports and dashboards** â€“ there is **no limit** on the number of reports a licensed user can build or view with the visual.

## 6. What happens if you run out of licences?

The behaviour depends on how your IT team has configured licence assignment, but typically:

- The system will **not automatically purchase new licences** unless it has been explicitly configured to do so.
- If a user attempts to use the visual **without a licence**, they may see an **message** in the visual prompting them to **purchase or request a licence**.
- In most standard setups, this **does not trigger an automatic purchase** of additional licences.

If youâ€™re unsure how your organisation handles this, we recommend checking with your **IT or licensing team**.

## 9. Quick FAQ

**How do we buy licences?**  
All purchases are made through **Microsoft AppSource** on the  
[Org Chart Drag and Drop]([YOUR_APPSOURCE_LISTING_URL_HERE](https://marketplace.microsoft.com/en-us/product/power-bi-visuals/workforcevision.org-chart-drag-and-drop?tab=PlansAndPrice)) listing.

**Who owns the licences â€“ users or the company?**  
Licences are owned by **your organisation** when purchased with a company bulling account. They can be **assigned, unassigned, and reassigned** by your IT/admin team.

**Can 1 licence cover multiple users?**  
No. Licences are **per user**. Each user who creates or uses the visual must have a licence assigned.

**Are licences tied to a specific workspace?**  
No. Licences are **assigned to users**. A licensed user can use the visual in any workspace they have permission to access.

**Can licences be used across tenants?**  
No. Licences **cannot be shared across tenants**.

**Can we change our plan mid-term?**  
You typically **cannot amend** a plan once it has started. Instead, cancel the existing plan and purchase a new one at the desired tier. There is **no cancellation fee**.

**Will the system automatically buy more licences if we run out?**  
Normally, **no**. Unless specifically configured, the system will not auto-purchase licences. Users without a licence may see an **message** prompting them to obtain one.
